Homemade Margherita Pizza

Enjoy a delicious Margherita Pizza and surprise all your loved ones in any special date. With this recipe you'll make anyone to love your cooking power.

Preparation time

  • Preparation Time (including dough rising): 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 12-15 minutes
  • Total Time: Approximately 1 hour 45 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Make a well in the center and pour in warm water and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or an upside-down baking sheet in the oven while preheating.
  3. Shape the Dough: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each portion of dough into a round shape (approximately 12 inches in diameter).
  4. Assemble the Pizza: Place each rolled-out dough on parchment paper. Spread marinara sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Arrange slices of fresh mozzarella cheese and tomato slices on top of the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza. Drizzle olive oil over the assembled pizza.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Carefully transfer the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Slice the pizza and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)
