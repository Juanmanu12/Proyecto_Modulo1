Vegetable Coconut Curry

Enjoy a delicious Vegetable Coconut Curry and surprise all your loved ones in any special date. With this recipe you'll make anyone to love your cooking power.

Preparation time

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: Approximately 40 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Curry Paste: In a small bowl, combine minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped onion, curry powder, ground turmeric, ground cumin, ground coriander, and red pepper flakes. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the prepared curry paste to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until fragrant.
  2. Cook the Vegetables: Add mixed vegetables to the skillet with the curry paste. Stir to coat the vegetables evenly with the paste. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables start to soften but still have a slight crunch.
  3. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  4. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
